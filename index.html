About AxeLab

Knight Out: Wave Fighter

One knight. Endless waves. Knight Out is a modular combat prototype built for replayability, challenge, and personality-driven AI. It’s a top-down survival experience where every wave teaches you something new. Branded UI, tight controls, and scalable systems—this is AxeLab’s flagship combat testbed.

Froggy Jump
